Trips

The Boy Scouts go on many trips to learn and have fun. There are national, regional, and troop trips. It teaches them how to survive in the wild, help others, and work together. There are also summer camps that help the scouts earn merit badges and ranks by having classes. Every camp out has a different experience. One example of a summer camp is Camp Mack Morris of West Tennessee Council. All the scouts have a lot of fun and can learn alot from the experience.
