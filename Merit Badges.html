Merit Badges

Merit badges are achievements that a scout can earn. They show a scouts knowledge. A scout can earn merit badges by working hard and completing challenges. There are exactly 136 merit badges that a scout can currently earn so it isn't easy to earn them all. In order to get some ranks a scout must earn a certain number of merit badges and some are required for eagle which is the highest rank for a scout. Scouts can earn more merit badges which go towards more accomplishments.

This boy scout is in his Eagle ceremony earning his Eagle rank. He is wearing a scout sash with every merit badge that he has earned to get to his Eagle rank and more.
